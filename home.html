Who is Elizabeth Warren?

Elizabeth Warren is a U.S. Senator for Massachusetts and running to be the 46th President of the United States. Born and raised in Oklohoma, she moved to Massachusetts to pursue a career in teaching. After the destruction caused by the 2008 financial crisis, she founded the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau where she helped millions of Americans recover from the predatory lending practices of banks at the time. In 2012 she defeated popular Republican incumbent Scott Brown to become a U.S. Senator.

Why is She Running?

Elizabeth Warren has proven she knows how to win against a popular Republican opponent. As a former Harvard professor and current U.S. Senator, she's also the most qualified person alive to lead this country. From Healthcare to Corporate Accountability, Elizabeth Warren has the plans to save our sinking middle class and finally hold the tiny sliver of wealth at the top accountable.
